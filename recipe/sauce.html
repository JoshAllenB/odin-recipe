Bang Bang Sauce

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes â€” almost everything!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoon sweet Thai chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

Directions

Step 1

Stir together mayonnaise, chili sauce, Sriracha, and rice vinegar in a small bowl until smooth.
